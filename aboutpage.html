Useful Cooking Tips

  • Measure ingredients correctly: Always use the correct measuring cups for liquid and dry ingredients.
  • Level off ingredients: For dry ingredients like flour or sugar, make sure to level off the top for accuracy.
  • Understanding conversions: Know the conversion rates when substituting ingredients to avoid mistakes.
  • Cooking at high altitudes: Cooking times and ingredient measurements may need adjustments at high altitudes.
